The Challenge

Sky was the first connected product to launch along with a new AWS platform. This allowed a direct connection to consumers and an unfiltered view from dealers to understand behaviors and how to continuously deliver value while gaining insights. The concept development began in 2015 and was launched in China in 2017.

There were complexities in tying services (i.e., authentication, registration, order management) across the organization. Understanding market constraints, such as what data was allowed to be accurately shown. We were the first product to carry forward the efforts to build the AWS platform that other products would also depend on for services such as security and data ponds.

Process

How We Approached It

We refined the initial concept from key screens to workflows and vetted them with our development team to confirm backend actions matched the frontend experience. We ran in sprints with quarterly planning with key releases.

Finding what could be measured and meaningfully presented to end users was a critical piece to gain traction.
